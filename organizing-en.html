Organizing

Together, we can co-create identities fit for purpose.

The environments we find ourselves in strongly affect much of our interactions and communication patterns. Those in turn shape our identity - and create new identities of teams and the entire organisation. That's what we call culture.

Organizing together we co-create identities fit for purpose.

The offer

Imagine a group of people ...
  • with a common mission
  • a clear strategy how to achieve the mission
  • strong human relationships & trust among each other
  • all the infrastructure and processes to enable fast action and effective decision making

Wanna stop them?

You might benefit

if you are
  • more than two people
  • struggling e.g. with mission creep, lack of strategy, lengthy decision making or other problems of organisation
  • willing to invest at least some time in self-exploration as a group or organization
  • open-minded as to what might be holding you back

What is organizing?

Organization happens -

whether you plan for it or not. So better be prepared and do it consciously.

I have seen in many contexts -

that people are perfectly capable of effective self-organization, forming functional organization from a group. Such organization creates new identities ('us' versus 'them'). Goals become entangled with organization ('we are the ones...') - except when there are no goals or people haven't bought into them.

I was taught -

in the fire pit of corporate dysfunction. Learned to look out for it.
