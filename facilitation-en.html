Facilitation

Let's invite to productive gatherings people want to come to.

I enjoy facilitating events for smart participants, who bring their energy and enjoy the work together. If you rely on people with these qualities, those are the ones who tend to show up. Surprise: nobody's grumpy, unhelpful or disheartened by nature.

  • the offer

The offer

Imagine a meeting in which the group ...
  • establishes a clear goal for the meeting
  • has ways of effective decision making
  • learns from its diversity
  • builds trust in its own capacities
  • follows up with meaningful action after the gathering

Sounds good?

You might benefit

if you are
  • clear on the purpose of your gathering - how is the world different after the meeting?
  • able to engage as a group - no mail check outside breaks...
  • willing to engage in some intentional play
