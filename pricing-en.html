pricing

Below you find my baseline rates for different services. But pricing really depends upon what you or your organziation can afford and what you do.

We'll figure out the details in our introductory call.

Details

Coaching

If you are in the top 10% of the income distribution, my usual rate is 100€/hr. ... This is tpyically the part of the population willing and able to pay for coaching services.

Pricing is adapted based on your monthly income. Don't worry, a rough estimate will do, I would never inquire into your financial situation in detail.

Facilitation

If you are a for-profit company or team with stable financial means, my usual rate is 960€/d for a full day 8h workshop. Travel costs are excluded; if you need me only for a few hours, the hourly rate is 120€/hr.

Pricing is adapted based on your mission and your means. If you are a non-profit organization, work for aspects of the common good, if you are an acitivist group of some kind, a club - in all those cases just get in touch.

Organizational consulting

If you are a for-profit company or team with stable financial means, my usual rate is 1.200€/d for a full work day. Travel costs are excluded; if you need me only for a few hours, the hourly rate is 150€/hr.

Pricing is adapted based on your mission and your means. If you are a non-profit organization, work for aspects of the common good, if you are an acitivist group of some kind, a club - in all those cases just get in touch.
